Before We Proceed, a Moment of Clarity

Greetings, intrepid traveler of the digital cosmos. You've stumbled upon a peculiar nexus of pixels and code, a portfolio masquerading as a familiar search engine. This is a grand illusion, a testament to the creative whims of a motion designer and web developer who enjoys a bit of playful deception.

By clicking 'Accept', you acknowledge that this is not the search engine you think it is. You agree not to hold us responsible if you find yourself questioning the nature of reality, the illusion of choice, or why you suddenly have the urge to learn more about this website. If you were looking for the actual Google, you've taken a wonderfully scenic detour.

Should you choose to 'Deny,' you'll be sent back to the void from whence you came (or, you know, the pop-up will just close). Either way, what is 'real' anyway? Just a series of electrical impulses interpreted by your brain.

Proceed with a sense of humor and an open mind. The rabbit hole is deep, but the website is smooth.
