11FS

A launchpad for the next generation of financial services.

I was hired by 11:FS to help move forward some key new features they were looking to white label to major banks. In this intense we were working with HSBC to help an internal push to help small to medium business owners find more security in payments and short term cash flow.

I worked with the UX team to produce an iOS app to help display how these new features could be implemented. I outlined the 3 journeys we wanted to use and helped to build high fidelity designs to help push the story of how users could take advantage of this.

Role: Senior Product Designer

Contribution: User Interface / Visual Design / Design Strategy / Presentation / Motion Design / User Experience
