The Search for Everything

Published on August 30, 2022 by Pushpak

Have you ever Googled yourself, half-curious, half-dreading what you might find? It’s a modern ritual of self-discovery. But it led me to a different question: What if you could design your own search result? What if your life was the search result, with all its complexities, side projects, and “People also ask” quirks?

Welcome to the answer. This portfolio, wrapped in the familiar comfort of the world’s biggest search engine. We turn to Google for answers, for clarity in a sea of information. This website is my attempt to provide a little clarity on the curated chaos of my own life, both for you and for myself. It’s a playful nod to the idea that we are all, in essence, searching for something.

Well, i’m a tech generalist, as I like to think of myself. My mind finds its joy in connecting seemingly unrelated things, the rigid logic of code with the fluid narrative of motion design, the quiet solitude of sketching with the collaborative energy of a web build.

My greatest breakthroughs don’t happen in brainstorming sessions, but in the quiet hum of a late night, switching from a complex animation timeline to a blank canvas, or getting lost in the impossible worlds of anime and sci-fi. It’s in that space between disciplines that the truly interesting ideas are born. This site is a map of that space.

So, what am I pursuing? What’s the ultimate query in this search?

It isn’t for a specific title or a corner office. My pursuit is for the right problems. I want to build the small universes people can get lost in. I want to craft the digital experiences that feel intuitive and human, and tell the visual stories that stick with you long after the screen goes dark. Whether it’s through code, animation, or design, the goal is the same: to take an idea, an emotion, or a complex thought and give it a tangible form that resonates with people.

This portfolio, this search result, is just a single entry. It’s a snapshot of what I am doing right now. But the query is my life, and the search is far from over.

Thanks for stopping by to see what I’ve done so far.
