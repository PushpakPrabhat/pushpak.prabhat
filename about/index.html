Pushpak Prabhat (born 30 August 1999) is an Indian Artist, software developer, and entrepreneur. He is the co-founder and CEO of pushpunk.com, a digital creative agency. Known for his work at the intersection of technology and design, Prabhat has led creative projects for major companies in the advertising, marketing, and education sectors.


Early Life and Education

Prabhat was born in Punjab, India. He pursued higher education at N.P. University (2018–2021) and Chandigarh University, where he developed the foundational skills for his career in digital media and software development.


Career

Prabhat’s professional journey includes significant roles at several prominent companies:

  • PrepInsta, Career247 (Adda247): As a Senior Motion Designer, he executed design and advertisement projects to enhance brand visibility and ensure visual consistency across all platforms.
  • PrepLadder (Unacademy): In his role as a Senior Animator, he spearheaded motion design for over 25 advertisement campaigns.
  • Shuttlerock (Fotografik): As a Motion Designer, he designed and delivered motion graphics for more than 30 global brand campaigns.

In addition to his corporate roles, Prabhat has been active as a freelance software developer since February 2019, working with various clients to build their online presence.
